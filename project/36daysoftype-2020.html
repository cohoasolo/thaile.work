Personal Project Digital Lettering

36daysoftype 2020

04/2020

After months of relaxation following the defense of my graduation thesis, I'm back in the lettering game with renewed energy. I participated in the 36 Days of Type (Edition 7, 2020) to create my own version of the Latin alphabet, numbers, and ampersand. During the 36-day challenge, I produced nine sketches for each letter every day.

One of those nine sketches was then refined and 3D rendered using Photoshop brushes. That's why you will see them in various styles but with the same overall effect.
