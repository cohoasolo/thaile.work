Hi - xin chào!

I’m Thai Le (b. 1998), a multidisciplinary designer based in Ho Chi Minh City. I’m drawn to projects that engage typography, culture, and the interplay between form and function. My practice is grounded in careful study and interpretation, shaped with clarity and attention to detail. Guided by a deep appreciation for beauty, I also explore motion, 3D, and creative coding as part of an expanding design practice.
