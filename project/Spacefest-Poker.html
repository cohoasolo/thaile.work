As we were getting close to Halloween, Maztermind had a very special product that was suitable to launch for this occasion. SPACEFEST was originally the name of a deck of playing cards, then expanded to be a poker set, with artworks created by the talented artist Supa Nhím collaborating with Maztermind's in-house product design team for the box.
Following the cosmic-carnivalic theme of the product, I translate the idea into art directions for the launching campaign, blending 2D, 3D and product photoshoot techniques into the design to achieve a surreal outcome.

Art Direction

Key Visual

People ususally relates the iris visual structure with the most beautiful and there's like a whole universe in it. Each one was crafted with extreme depth and sophistication. The idea behind the KV was to link SPACEFEST Poker with a visual of an eye from outer space, and the product itself was the iris of it. While letting the lid open up a little bit, I wanted to convey a breaking of ordinary rule, just like the campaign tagline meant.
